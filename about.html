About Caesar Cypher

Instructions:


Encoding:

To encode a message, simply put it in the text area, choose a key between 0 and 26 and click encode!

Decoding with a key:

Simply put it in the text area, put the key in the correct box and click decode!

Decoding without a key:

Put it in the text area and click the Get Key button. If the message has more than 10 characters, it will put what the program thinks is the correct key in the Key box. Simply click decode afterwards!
